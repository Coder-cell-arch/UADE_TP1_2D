Unity version: 6000.0.30f1

How to open:
1) Clone the repo or Download ZIP and extract.
2) In Unity Hub: Open the repo folder (you must see Assets/Packages/ProjectSettings at the root).
3) Open Assets/Scenes/GameScene.unity and press Play.

Controls:
- ENTER: start (initial screen)
- A/D: move
- SPACE: double jump
- C: melee

Notes:
- Tilemap uses TilemapCollider2D + Rigidbody2D (Static).
- Dummy has Idle/Hit animations and reacts to the player's melee.
